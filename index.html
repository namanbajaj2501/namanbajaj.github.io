Hi, I'm Naman

Astronomer specializing in planet formation and protoplanetary disk evolution.

I am an astronomer studying how planets form and evolve within protoplanetary disks around young stars. My research combines observations and theory to understand disk evolution, gas dispersal, and the conditions under which planetary systems emerge.

I am particularly interested in the final stages of planet formation and how disk winds and gas loss shape planetary architectures.

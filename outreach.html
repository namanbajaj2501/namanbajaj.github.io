Science Outreach

Science isn't complete until it's shared. I'm passionate about making astronomy accessible and inspiring the next generation of scientists. From podcasts to classrooms to press coverage, I believe in sharing all our amazing discoveries about the universe.

Podcasts & Interviews

I've had the privilege of sharing my research journey and discoveries through some podcast appearances. These conversations cover everything from my path from India to NASA-funded astronomy research to the latest JWST discoveries about how planets form.

Podcasts allow me to connect with diverse audiences‚Äîfrom aspiring scientists wondering if astronomy is for them, to curious listeners who just want to understand what we're learning about the universe. It's storytelling at its best, and I love breaking down complex science into relatable stories.

School Outreach

One of the most rewarding parts of being a scientist is inspiring the next generation. I regularly visit schools to talk about astronomy, perform some live demonstrations, and answer their big, heavy, and difficult questions!

Whether I'm explaining how planets form to elementary school students using everyday analogies or discussing career paths with high schoolers curious about astronomy, these interactions remind me why I fell in love with science in the first place. Seeing a student's eyes light up when they understand a concept, or hearing "I didn't know I could do that", is what keeps me going.
